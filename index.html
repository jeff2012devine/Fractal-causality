Plain-Language Introduction

Fractal Causality is a hypothesis that the universe carries a hidden rhythm — a repeating log-periodic pattern — that appears across multiple scales. From the clustering of galaxies, to the hum of gravitational waves and atomic clocks, to predictive signals in the human brain, the model proposes that a single underlying “oscillator” leaves its fingerprints everywhere.

Version 3.2 formalizes this into a testable framework with precise equations, falsifiability criteria, and reproducibility protocols.
