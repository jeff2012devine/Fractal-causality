overview

fractal causality proposes that black holes are not endpoints but transformation chambers where holographically stored information undergoes a loop-quantum-gravity bounce and a conformal flip, re-seeding new expansion with structured initial conditions. version 3.3 provides a minimal mathematical model, stability criteria, and falsifiable predictions across cmb, lss, gws, and 21 cm cosmology.
